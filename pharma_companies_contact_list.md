## Recommended Approach:
1. **Start with the top 6** companies listed above (verified email addresses)
2. **Send personalized emails** using the template provided
3. **For other companies**, visit their official websites to find contact forms or email addresses
4. **Follow up** after 5-7 business days
5. **Keep records** of all communications

## Notes:
- Some emails are for specific departments (medical info, complaints, etc.)
- For supplier/distributor inquiries, use **General Inquiries** or **Corporate Affairs** emails
- Always include your pharmacy license and business registration when requested
- Be patient - responses may take 1-2 weeks
