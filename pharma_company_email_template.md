# Email Template for Pharmaceutical Companies

## Subject Line Options:
1. Request for Supplier/Distributor Contact Information - [Your Medical Store Name]
2. Partnership Inquiry: Medical Store Seeking Direct Supplier Contacts
3. Request for Authorized Distributor Details

---

## Email Template:

**Subject:** Request for Supplier/Distributor Contact Information - [Your Medical Store Name]

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hope this email finds you well.

My name is [Your Name], and I am writing to you on behalf of **[Your Medical Store Name]**, a registered medical store located in [Your City/Area], Pakistan. We have been serving our community for [duration] and are committed to providing quality pharmaceutical products to our customers.

We are currently in the process of streamlining our supply chain management and would greatly appreciate your assistance in obtaining the contact information for your authorized distributors/suppliers in our region.

**Specifically, we would like to request:**
- Names and contact details of your authorized distributors/suppliers serving [Your City/Region]
- Contact person names and phone numbers
- Email addresses for placing orders
- Any specific ordering procedures or requirements

This information will help us ensure that we are sourcing your products through proper channels and maintaining the quality standards that both our organizations uphold.

We are particularly interested in establishing a reliable supply chain for the following product categories:
- [List specific categories, e.g., Analgesics, Antibiotics, Cardiovascular medications, etc.]

If you require any documentation from our end (such as our pharmacy license, NTN, or business registration), please let us know, and we will be happy to provide it.

Thank you for your time and consideration. We look forward to your positive response and the opportunity to strengthen our business relationship.

Best regards,

**[Your Name]**  
[Your Position]  
[Your Medical Store Name]  
[Complete Address]  
[Phone Number]  
[Your Email Address]  
[Store License Number, if applicable]

## Alternative: Shorter Version

**Subject:** Request for Authorized Distributor Contacts

Dear Team,

I am [Your Name] from [Your Medical Store Name], a registered pharmacy in [City], Pakistan.

We would like to establish a direct supply chain for your pharmaceutical products and kindly request the contact information of your authorized distributors/suppliers serving our area.

Please share:
- Distributor names and contact details
- Order placement procedures
- Any required documentation from our end

Thank you for your assistance.

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Contact Information]

---

## Tips for Sending:
1. **Personalize** each email with the company name
2. **Attach** your pharmacy license and business registration (if requested)
3. **Follow up** after 5-7 business days if no response
4. **Be professional** and concise
5. **Use your official** medical store email address
